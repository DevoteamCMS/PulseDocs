# Information Security Policy

## Purpose

The policy directs the design, implementation, and management of an effective Information Security Program for Devoteam Cloud Managed Services. It ensures that "information assets are appropriately identified, recorded, and always afforded suitable protection."

Key objectives include:

- Protecting Company Information from unauthorized disclosure or access
- Providing executive direction for information security management aligned with business requirements, laws, and regulations
- Delivering risk-based information security controls

## Goals and Objectives

**Compliance**
- Conduct annual internal compliance audits to verify ISO 27001 adherence
- Address non-compliance issues within 90 days of identification

**Security Awareness and Training**
- Ensure 100% of employees complete annual security awareness training
- Conduct regular phishing simulations to reduce click-through rates by 50%

**Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery**
- Test disaster recovery procedures annually and update as needed
- Maintain resilience through effective continuity and recovery plans

**Access Control**
- Implement multi-factor authentication across critical systems within six months
- Achieve 100% adherence to role-based access control policies

**Incident Response**
- Detect and resolve 90% of incidents within specified SLA (within 24 hours)

**Risk Management**
- Conduct annual risk assessments with treatment plans implemented within 60 days

## Scope

This policy covers all Company information and systems used, managed, or operated by employees, contractors, and vendors for cloud-based solutions, services, and operations.

## Policy Statement

The organization commits to three principles:

- **Confidentiality**: Information accessible only to authorized individuals
- **Integrity**: Accuracy and completeness of information maintained
- **Availability**: Information accessible to authorized users when required

"Devoteam Cloud Managed Services has implemented an Information Security Management System based on the ISO/IEC 27001:2022 International Standard."

Technical and organizational security measures protect information against unauthorized access, deletion, loss, or misuse.
